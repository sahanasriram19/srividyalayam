What We Offer

Our Classes

Every class at Srividyalayam is taught with personal attention and rooted in authentic Carnatic tradition. We welcome students of all ages — from young children taking their first steps to adults returning to music.

Carnatic Vocal
Classical South Indian Singing

Explore the vast world of Carnatic music through your voice. Lessons cover sruti, laya, and raga — beginning with foundational swaras and progressing to kritis, varnams, and concert-level performance.

  • Notation identification
  • Manodharma sangeetham
  • Extensive repertoire of Kritis/compositions
  • Rhythm & tala training
  • Performance preparation

Carnatic Violin
Classical String Mastery

The violin holds a unique and cherished place in Carnatic music. Learn proper bowing technique, gamaka, and the nuances of Carnatic intonation — whether your goal is solo performance or accompanying other musicians.

  • Posture, tuning & bow technique
  • Gamaka & fingering techniques
  • Scale patterns & raga grammar
  • Accompaniment skills
  • Solo performance development
