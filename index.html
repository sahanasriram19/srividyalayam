About the School

A New Home for
Classical Music

Srividyalayam Music School was born from a deep love for the Carnatic tradition. Though newly opened, it is built on a foundation of over two decades of dedicated teaching experience — bringing that same passion, rigour, and warmth to every student who walks through our doors.

We honour the ancient guru-shishya tradition, where music is not merely taught but transmitted — from heart to heart, with patience and care. Every student is seen as an individual, and every lesson is shaped around their journey.

Whether you are a curious beginner or a returning musician, Srividyalayam is your home.
